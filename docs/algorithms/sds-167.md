Secondary target: Goethals-Seidel construction over `Z_167`.

- exact enumeration of the 10 feasible `(k1, k2, k3, k4; lambda)` parameter sets forced by the row-sum square constraint

- cyclic SDS block representation and difference-profile math
- shardable meet-in-the-middle SDS search scaffold, currently exercised on a small `Z_5` instance via `hadamard search sds --order 5 --block-sizes 2,2,0,0 --lambda 1`
